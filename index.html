M2Doc

generates Office Open XML documents from models

Download

 

Visual template

The overall approach consists in creating templates in the OOX format where static text authoring benefits from the visual capabilities of the usual tools (e.g. Microsoft Word, Libre Office, Open Office)

Dynamic content

During the document generation, dynamic parts are inserted using a dedicated simple vocabulary.
